#### Methodology

```

```

For $37.3B market cap to be justified:

```
Terminal FCF = $299M × (1+3%) / (12%-3%) = $3.42B

**Sum of PV of FCFs:**
```

**This still doesn't match. The issue is terminal value needs to be much larger.**

### Appendix F: Glossary of Terms

**DCF (Discounted Cash Flow):** Valuation method based on present value of future cash flows

**EV/Sales (Enterprise Value to Sales):** Valuation multiple comparing company's total value to revenue

**EBITDA:** Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization

**FCF (Free Cash Flow):** Cash generated after capital expenditures

**LEO (Low Earth Orbit):** Altitude 200-2,000 km, most common satellite orbit

**P/S Ratio (Price-to-Sales):** Market cap divided by annual revenue

**SSO (Sun-Synchronous Orbit):** Specific type of polar orbit at ~500-600 km

**WACC (Weighted Average Cost of Capital):** Discount rate representing company's cost of funding

**GTO (Geostationary Transfer Orbit):** Elliptical orbit used to reach geostationary orbit

**ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations):** US export controls on defense technology
